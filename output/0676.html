All accepted papers will be indexed by both EI Compendex and ISTP/ISI Proceedings, as well as included in the IEEE Xplore.
        
OVERVIEW

The annual Wireless and Optical Communications Conference (WOCC) aims to provide a forum for exchanging information among, professionals from Mainland China, Taiwan, Hong-Kong, the United States and other counties and regions on the progress of technologies in the wireless and optical communications arena.
 
The conference dated back to 1992, was initiated by Chinese professionals in the optical and wireless communication fields in the United States with the support of major Chinese-American professional organizations. Through two decades, WOCC has expanded to be an international conference with its main objective to strengthen the technical and business relationships between the Asian, North American and other regions in the world.
 
Currently WOCC is rotating among US-Mainland China-US-Taiwan (China) in a yearly basis with 200+ attendees at each meeting.
 
The 22nd WOCC will be held from 16th to 18th , May 2013 in Chongqing, China.
 
 
Original technical papers are solicited on the topics including, but not limited to, the following:
