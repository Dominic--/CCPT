Capture your audience's attention.

Most people decide within a few seconds if a website is useful to them. Use
these tips to see which content on your site turns visitors into customers.

Access your account
<https://www.google.com/analytics/web/?utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_campaign=febbasic&utm_content=en_us>

Give people what they want

Customers come to your website with a simple goal: to find what they're
looking for. And if they don't find it, they'll look elsewhere. Get more
sales or leads by putting relevant information front-and-center.

Visit the All Pages report in the 'Content' section of your Analytics
account to find out what content clicks most with your customers.
<https://www.google.com/analytics/web/?utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_campaign=febbasic&utm_content=en_us#report/content-pages/>

You'll see:

- Which of your website pages are most popular

- Where people spend the most and least time on your site

- Which pages users leave your site from (i.e. have a high 'bounce'
or 'exit rate')

Test for success

Improve the performance of important pages on your site by using Content
Experiments. Try out two different versions of a page to see which one
drives more sales or leads-for example, if you want to get more leads
through your sales team, test a version with the 'Contact Us' button
featured more prominently.
<https://www.google.com/analytics/web/?utm_medium=email&utm_source=newsletter&utm_campaign=febbasic&utm_content=en_us#report/siteopt-experiments/>
